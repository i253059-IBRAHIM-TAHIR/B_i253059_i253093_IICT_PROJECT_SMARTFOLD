Shipping policy

Although we primarily operate in Canada and the United States, we do have the ability to ship most products worldwide, including Europe and Asia. Please note, some products and countries require custom quotes to determine shipping costs. If you're shopping from outside North America, please contact us by phone or email; once we've determined the shipping cost to your location, we can process your order.

Certain larger items, such as Italian Wall Beds or MurphySofa Wall beds, require custom shipping quotes. We reserve the right to charge additional shipping, in rare circumstances such as a product being shipped to a remote location. Please contact us for more details.

Most products will be delivered inside your home, provided you have an elevator or ground access; however, sometimes curbside is our only option without excess fees. If you have stairs, please let us know prior to placing your order.

The Types of Beds We Design

Our designers don’t just think of a bed as a place to sleep, they need to be futureproof pieces of furniture that work with your space today and 10 years from now while providing a feeling of comfort so you can rest easily at night.  That’s why we take extra care with every detail from the flush in a cabinet to the mechanics that make it simple to lower, extend, and rotate your bed into place.

Every detail matters including enhanced storage, maximizing living spaces when your bed isn’t in use, and creating transformative designs to meet your needs from small apartments to expansive master bedrooms.   

Here at Expand you’ll find the following types of beds:

  • Bunk beds

  • Hidden beds

  • Lift beds

  • Murphy beds

  • Sofa beds

Bunk beds

Perfect for guest rooms, shared bedrooms, and rental homes, you can choose from options that attach to the wall, extend from a couch, or fold seamlessly along an office wall and lower horizontally when you need to maximize efficiency.  

Hidden beds

Studio apartments are notoriously tricky to sleep a guest overnight, same with smaller homes that don’t have a guest room, and even a vacation home where you want to increase capacity.  That’s where our line of hidden beds comes in.

We created a couch that transforms into three separate beds, wall panels that lower into a bunk bed, and compact cabinets that are stunning storage spaces and decor during the day that lower like a traditional murphy to sleep a guest at night.  

Lift beds

When you want extra storage space, a lift bed is for you.  Through our innovative mechanics and custom designs, you can lift the bed by yourself revealing ample storage space making it easier to access decor items, clothing, or anything else under your bed.  

There’s no more struggling to reach under a traditional bed frame, our lift bed technology provides you with a simple way to access your storage space without the hassle.

Murphy beds

Our most popular and in-demand style, each murphy bed from Expand Furniture is made to enhance your space including options with shelves, desks and tables, or that double as a sofa on one side.  We took the traditional cabinet and turned it into functional furniture that not only looks great, it provides your room with more function.

Whether you choose a murphy bed that rotates or lowers, each one is able to be converted from furniture to a sleeping space quickly and by a single person.  Say goodbye to chunk cabinets and hello to style and sophistication with our line of murphy beds. 

Sofa beds

We’ve reinvented the sofa bed by turning the boring and multi-step system your parents had into showstopping pieces of furniture.  Some of our options lift up for a double decker couch with a sleeping space, and others morph from a seating area to a king size sleeping space fit for royalty.

We even have a sleeper sofa that houses three separate beds including a rollaway, a bunk bed couch, and standard bed in the main frame.  Each one is comfortable to let you or your guests get a great night sleep while the transformations become talking pieces to delight your guests vs. a simple sleeper sofa that they were expecting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver inside? Up stairs?

Yes most products will be delivered inside your home provided you have an elevator or ground access, sometimes curbside is our only option without excess fees. If you have stairs please let us know ahead of time before you order as we need to make special arrangements.

Warranty policy?

Lifetime Warranty on telescopic table mechanisms.

5 year warranty store wide on all hardware parts on all Expand Furniture products.

We pride ourselves with exceptional warranty offerings that go far beyond industry standards.
We do this because we believe we have better products and ones that will last.

What is your return policy?

7 day trial with easy returns on most products
We expect our customers will love our products as much as we do, however we understand things won’t always work as planned so we created a flexible policy. Details below.

Returns: For USA & Canadian customers we accept returns on all stocked tables, chairs and sofa modules provided we are notified within 7 days of delivery. Original packaging is required.

Ineligible items: We do not accept returns on custom order products, clearance items, or items that can be slept on including Murphy Beds (wall beds), mattresses.

Return fees: We charge a simple 15% fee on any returned item. Shipping costs are not refundable.

See our full policy here

What is the Quality Guarantee?

We pride ourselves on the quality of every item we carry, we work very hard to make sure we only have the best. We back this up by offering lifetime warranties on our telescopic table and Murphy Bed Frame mechanisms as well as an exceptional 5 year store wide warranty on all product hardware.

Even if somehow something went wrong after the warranty period is over, we will be happy to try and send any replacement parts at cost, or for free if we are able to. Most products are built to be modular and repairable to extend the product lifecycle. We carry spare parts and if we don’t have it in stock, often we can make the parts needed for your product. Rest assured with our satisfaction guarantee, if you aren’t overjoyed with something, let us know and we will be at your service.
